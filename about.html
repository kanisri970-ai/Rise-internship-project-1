Who We Are

Born from a Passion for Better Shopping

ShopWave was founded in 2020 in Hosur, Tamil Nadu, with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to high-quality products at honest prices, without the hassle of traditional retail.

From a small warehouse with 50 products, we've grown to over 10,000 SKUs across categories — all while keeping our promise of authenticity, fast delivery, and genuine customer care at the heart of everything we do.
