Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery (select areas) is available in 24–48 hours for a small additional fee.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy on all products. Items must be in original condition with packaging intact. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.

Are the products authentic?

Absolutely. Every product listed on ShopWave goes through our authentication process. We only partner with verified brands and sellers.
